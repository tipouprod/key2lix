Buying

How do I buy a digital item?+
Use the search bar or browse categories. Add the item to your cart, choose a payment method, and complete the purchase.
What is Key2lix Plus?+
Key2lix Plus is our membership program offering extra discounts and exclusive offers on many digital items.

Digital Items

How do I claim a purchased item?+
Items are delivered instantly via email or your Key2lix Dashboard.
How do I check the platform or region of a digital item?+
Each product page shows the platform (PC, Xbox, PlayStation) and the region compatibility. Make sure to select the correct version before purchase.

Payments

What payment methods are available?+
Key2lix supports credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other regional payment options depending on your location.
Can I get a refund?+
Refunds depend on seller policies. Open a support ticket with your order ID for assistance.

Orders

How do I track my order?+
Go to Dashboard > My Orders to track processing and delivery.
How long will it take to receive my item?+
Most items are delivered instantly. Some items may take up to 24 hours depending on the seller.

Account

How do I reset my password?+
Click Forgot Password on the login page and follow the instructions.
How do I delete my account?+
To close your account, contact Key2lix Support via email or live chat. They will guide you through the process.

Shipping & Delivery

Do all items deliver instantly?+
Most digital items are delivered instantly via email. Some sellers may require up to 24 hours.

Security & Safety

Is it safe to buy on Key2lix?+
Yes, Key2lix uses secure payment gateways and encryption to protect your personal data.
How do I avoid scams or fake sellers?+
Always check seller ratings, reviews, and choose offers with instant delivery. Avoid deals outside the platform.
